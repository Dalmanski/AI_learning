How to learn AI?

What should I command to AI?

  • forget "(specific to remove)"
    • Example: forget "hi"
  • replace on "(specific)"" to "(anything)"
    • Example: replace on "hi" to "eyo"
  • show me your data
    • This will be shown all of data of specific AI
